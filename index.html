  • Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

    • Progressive enhancement is a form of web design where web content is put first. This design method is used to make sure the content your adding your style ontop off is up to par. As companies build their own websites many focus on how to manipulate people into using their site or buy their product without thinking of how the site looks. Progressive enhancement causes you to plan out your site before you begin working thus making the end product better. It's important to use this method as people will generally not trust a site that is not very well put together. If a website looks untrustworthy they will fell less inclined to buy something or give out information. To get the best results and most usable websites for your purposes using the progressive enhancement method is a very good way to do that.
